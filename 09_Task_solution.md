# Task 9 — Events and Probabilities in Weekly Weather

Each day

$$
P(S)=P(C)=P(R)=\frac13
$$

Total weekly outcomes

$$
|\Omega_7|=3^7
$$

---

### Weekend Sunny

$$
P(A)=\left(\frac13\right)^2=\frac19
$$

---

### Wed–Fri Rainy

$$
P(B)=\left(\frac13\right)^3=\frac1{27}
$$

---

### At Least One Sunny Day

$$
P(C)=1-\left(\frac23\right)^7
$$

---

### No Rainy Day

Only S or C allowed

$$
P(D)=\left(\frac23\right)^7
$$

---

### Exactly Two Sunny Days

$$
P(E)=\binom72\left(\frac13\right)^2\left(\frac23\right)^5
$$

---

### Additional Event

Event: **all week cloudy**

$$
P=\left(\frac13\right)^7
$$
