# Task 6 — Events and Probabilities in Coin Tossing

## Useful Formula

For equally likely outcomes

$$
P(A) = \frac{|A|}{|\Omega|}
$$

---

# Elementary Outcome Probabilities

For a fair coin

## One Toss

$$
\Omega_1 = \{H,T\}
$$

$$
P(H)=P(T)=\frac12
$$

---

## Two Tosses

$$
\Omega_2=\{HH,HT,TH,TT\}
$$

Each outcome

$$
P=\frac14
$$

---

## Three Tosses

$$
|\Omega_3| = 8
$$

Each outcome

$$
P=\frac18
$$

---

# One Toss

### Event \(A_1\): Heads

$$
A_1=\{H\}
$$

$$
P(A_1)=\frac12
$$

### Event \(B_1\): Tails

$$
P(B_1)=\frac12
$$

### Event \(C_1\): Not Tails

$$
P(C_1)=\frac12
$$

---

# Two Tosses

### Exactly One Head

$$
A_2=\{HT,TH\}
$$

$$
P(A_2)=\frac24=\frac12
$$

### At Least One Head

$$
B_2=\{HH,HT,TH\}
$$

$$
P(B_2)=\frac34
$$

### Same Result

$$
C_2=\{HH,TT\}
$$

$$
P(C_2)=\frac24=\frac12
$$

---

# Three Tosses

### Exactly Two Heads

$$
A_3=\{HHT,HTH,THH\}
$$

$$
P(A_3)=\frac38
$$

### At Least One Tail

$$
P(B_3)=1-\frac18=\frac78
$$

### All Same

$$
C_3=\{HHH,TTT\}
$$

$$
P(C_3)=\frac28=\frac14
$$

---

### Additional Event

Event: **exactly one tail**

$$
D_3=\{HHT,HTH,THH\}
$$

$$
P(D_3)=\frac38
$$
